--- title: Cross-section tool shortdesc: Use the CrossSectionTool to visualize cross-sections. longdesc: The CrossSectionTool helps managing features related to cross-sections and clipping planes, such as translation/rotation of the plane, adding and removing objects to clip, and enabling cross-section visualization through the stencil buffer. Keep in mind that clipping planes are a purely graphical mechanism that does not actually change what is loaded in the scene. In other words, an object can be entirely clipped and still keeping on processing its data normally. attribution: © Mapbox, © IGN tags: [clipping] ---
